Gemini AI Credentials

ShopSnap uses the Gemini API client-side to recognize items from your camera photos.

How to get a free API Key:

  1. Go to Google AI Studio.
  2. Sign in with your Google account.
  3. Click "Get API key" at the top left.
  4. Create a key and paste it into the field above.
  5. Ensure you save the key. It resides only on your device.
